# OpenPGxLab Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned structure of the OpenPGxLab learning path.

## Phase 1 — Foundations

### Module 1. Foundations of PGx
- What PGx is and what it is not
- Pharmacogenetics vs pharmacogenomics
- Pharmacokinetics vs pharmacodynamics
- Why drug response differs between patients
- Efficacy, toxicity, and adverse reaction concepts
- Introductory examples

### Module 2. Core PGx Genes and Mechanisms
- CYP enzymes
- Drug metabolism basics
- Prodrugs vs active drugs
- Transporters and targets
- Major PGx genes
- Phenoconversion and drug-drug interactions

### Module 3. HLA and Immune-Mediated PGx
- HLA overview
- Hypersensitivity mechanisms
- Immune-mediated adverse drug reactions
- HLA typing in PGx
- Interpretation challenges

## Phase 2 — Interpretation and Technology

### Module 4. Genotype-to-Phenotype Interpretation
- Variants, haplotypes, and diplotypes
- Star allele nomenclature
- Activity score systems
- Metabolizer categories
- Rare and uncertain alleles
- Limits of interpretation

### Module 5. PGx Testing Technologies
- Targeted PGx panels
- SNP arrays
- WES
- WGS
- Short-read sequencing
- Long-read sequencing
- HLA typing technologies
- Technology selection by use case

### Module 6. PGx Bioinformatics
- PGx pipeline design
- Variant types relevant to PGx
- Haplotype and star allele calling
- CNVs and structural variants
- Hard loci such as CYP2D6
- HLA typing approaches
- Short-read vs long-read analysis

## Phase 3 — Decision Support and Implementation

### Module 7. Annotation Databases and Decision Support
- PharmGKB
- CPIC
- DPWG
- PharmVar
- ClinVar and complementary resources
- Population databases
- Local annotation strategies

### Module 8. Clinical Reporting and Implementation
- Actionable findings
- From genotype to recommendation
- Dose adjustment and alternative drug logic
- Contraindications and warnings
- Reporting structure
- Workflow integration

### Module 9. Population-Specific and Real-World PGx
- Population differences
- Underrepresented populations
- Assay design considerations
- Validation and implementation strategy

### Module 10. Advanced Topics and Future Directions
- Oncology PGx vs somatic biomarkers
- AI-assisted interpretation
- Platform design
- Research directions
- Community contributions

## Hands-On Track

The project will also include practical content such as:

- case-based exercises
- genotype-to-phenotype examples
- technology selection exercises
- HLA interpretation cases
- annotation workflow exercises
- pipeline design exercises

## Current Priority

Current work is focused on:

1. defining the repository structure  
2. writing the core modules  
3. preparing introductory learning material  
4. building the first practical exercises
